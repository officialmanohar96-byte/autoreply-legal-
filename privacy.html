Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08 Sept 2025

AutoReply Pro ("we", "us", "our") provides an AI-based service to auto-reply to comments and DMs on Instagram, Facebook and YouTube. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, name) when you sign up.
  • OAuth tokens and limited profile data from Facebook/Instagram/YouTube only with your permission.
  • Basic analytics and usage logs for improving service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To fetch comments/DMs and post replies on your connected social accounts.
  • To manage subscriptions and billing (e.g., via Razorpay).
  • To improve and personalize the service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with payment providers (Razorpay) as required for billing.

4. Data Retention & Security

OAuth tokens are stored encrypted. You can disconnect accounts anytime. We follow industry-standard security practices.

5. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.
